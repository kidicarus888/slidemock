Expanded Strategic Value

Strategic Value for Payer-Investors

  • MLR Optimization: Direct reduction in Medical Loss Ratio via complication avoidance and unit cost arbitrage.
  • Prediabetes Reversal: Stopping the progression from prediabetes to chronic diabetes, eliminating future high-cost claimants.
  • Fraud & Waste Control: The "Gatekeeper" model eliminates duplicate testing, phantom billing, and provider shopping.
  • Actuarial Feedback Loop: Real-time clinical data flows back to underwriting, improving risk pricing accuracy.

For Patients (Retention)

  • Seamless Experience: One provider record managing the entire journey across labs, pharmacy, and specialists.
  • Better Access: National coverage via partner labs and pharmacies with cashless convenience.
  • Proactive Care: Adjustments made *before* issues become emergencies.
  • Plan Stickiness: High-touch care creates high switching costs, reducing policy churn.

For Loop (Asset Efficiency)

  • Asset-Light Scaling: Expand nationally without building clinics or labs (high ROIC).
  • High Margin: Revenue from clinical management fees, not just low-margin fulfillment.
  • Defensibility: Deep integration with payer claims workflows creates high barriers to entry.
  • Data Moat: Proprietary ML models trained on unique local metabolic risk profiles.
Why Market Entry Makes Sense
1. Clear Pipeline (Zero CAC) Unlike B2C apps fighting for users, our pipeline is clear and guaranteed. Access to TPA claims data and insurance policy mandates allows us to enroll high-need members with near-zero acquisition cost.
2. Perfect Incentive Alignment The ecosystem welcomes this entry because incentives are finally aligned. Payers reduce claims liability, Providers (partners) get volume, and Loop captures value by orchestrating the efficiency.
This roadmap upgrades Loop from a digital service into a clinic-led metabolic platform that insurers can route members into and scale.
